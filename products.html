Our standard

One rule, applied to every product in the catalogue.

❌ Doesn't make it into Genki

  • Palm oil
  • High-fructose corn syrup (HFCS)
  • Artificial sweeteners
  • Artificial colourings
  • Hydrogenated fats
  • MSG / E621

✅ Makes it into Genki

  • 100% made in Bulgaria
  • Clean, readable ingredients
  • Selected from 30+ trusted producers
  • Seasonally refreshed mix
  • Hand-curated for every client
  • Never a one-size-fits-all catalogue

If a product doesn't meet the standard, it doesn't go into the station. No exceptions.

How your assortment comes together

No generic stocking. Every Genki corner is shaped around your team and kept fresh.

01

Tell us about your team

Dietary needs, favourites and dislikes, allergies, vegan / gluten-free / nut-free. A 30-minute consultation. We listen more than we talk.

02

We curate the assortment

We curate from 30+ trusted Bulgarian producers. We balance familiar favourites (70%) with new discoveries (30%). Never a one-size-fits-all catalogue — every corner is different.

03

Fresh deliveries, seasonal rotation

An agreed schedule. Each season we refresh 10–15% of the mix with new products. Nothing sits, nothing gets boring.
