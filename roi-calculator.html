How is this calculated?

Sizing the station

The station is sized by how many people are physically in the office on a typical day — not by total headcount. Hybrid and remote patterns mean a 265-person company often has far fewer people on-site at once.

Per covered employee

The monthly price (excl. VAT) is the live price of the recommended station. Per covered employee spreads that price across your whole headcount — everyone benefits from the station, whichever day they come in.

This is a capacity and pricing tool — no savings or ROI projections. For reference, Multisport runs €20–51 per employee per month.
