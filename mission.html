Healthy people. Local producers. Real charity.

Three commitments built into every Genki order — to your team, to Bulgarian producers, and to causes that matter.

Genki is not product delivery. Not a vending machine. Not catering. Genki is managed wellness infrastructure — built with premium hardware, stocked with carefully selected Bulgarian products, and designed around long-term care for people in the office.

01
FIRST PRINCIPLE

Only carefully selected products.

Every product goes through the same strict filter — it either meets the standard or it doesn't enter Genki. We limit products with artificial colors, aggressive sweeteners, hydrogenated fats, and heavily processed low-value ingredients.

Our catalog is built around cleaner ingredients, better nutritional profiles, and products people genuinely want to consume daily at work.

TREAT Waffles, cookies, chocolate treats
CRUNCH Breadsticks, crackers, baked snacks
REFRESH Kombucha, cold teas, functional and energy drinks
02
SECOND PRINCIPLE

100% made in Bulgaria.

Roobar is sold in more than 50 countries. Smart Organic is present in Whole Foods USA and Selfridges UK. Bulgaria already produces world-class products — Genki simply places them where people spend their day.

We support local production, shorter supply chains, and Bulgarian businesses operating at international standards.

03
THIRD PRINCIPLE

10% of profit. Every month.

Not a percentage of revenue. Not a marketing number. Ten percent of real profit — after costs, salaries, and taxes — goes toward Bulgarian causes. Every month. With transparency and accountability to every client. This isn't a campaign. It's part of how Genki exists.
